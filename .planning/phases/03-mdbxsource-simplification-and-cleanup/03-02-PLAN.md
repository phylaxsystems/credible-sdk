<objective>
Reduce the MDBX circular buffer depth from the config-driven value to 1 in the embedded state
worker's StateWriter construction. With CommitHead flow control in place, MDBX writes never
advance past commit_head — the "went too far" scenario that required depth > 1 is architecturally
impossible. Depth 1 eliminates the namespace rotation overhead and simplifies the storage model.

Purpose: Completes SIMP-02 on the write side; the read side (StateReader) is handled by Plan 01.
Output: build_worker() always opens StateWriter with CircularBufferConfig::new(1).
</objective>
